Why are Spanish Songs so Fire?

Growing up as a hispanic child, I was forced to listen to Mexican songs when we were in the car. At first I did not like listening to these songs, but you never really appreciate things until you are older. The issue was that I did not understand the songs as I do now. These songs are so fucking fire. Nothing but straight bangers, and alot of this has to do with Spanish being such a good language. It feels like emotions are conveyed better and with much deeper feelings. Like I will hear a heartbreak or sad song and just feel it much more than I would if the song was in English. Anyway here is a list of Spanish songs that I love listening to at the moment in no particular order.

  • TÚ NAME by Fuerza Regida
  • LAGUNAS by Peso Pluma, Jasiel Nuñez
  • Corazón de Piedra by Xavi
  • CRAZYZ by Fuerza Regida
  • Mi Bello Angel by Natanael Cano
  • Ya No Somos Ni Seremos by Christian Nodal
  • La Intención by Christian Nodal, Peso Pluma
  • Que Onda by Calle 24, Chino Pacas, Fuerza Regida
  • Madonna by Natanael Cano, Oscar Maydon
  • DE LUNES A LUNES by Grupo Frontera, Manuel Turizo
